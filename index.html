Why I Love Detrtoit

Detroit is a city located in Michigan where you can view Canada directly across the river.I was born and raised in Detroit. I have alot of family that still live there.I Lived in Detroit for over thirty years, also I did most of my education there. The crime rate is high for an mostly all African american city. Many races are begining to relocate their now,and the city just elected it's first white Mayor in over several decades. Emploment,resources and education are among the lowest in the country, but it's a place where you can have lots of fun
