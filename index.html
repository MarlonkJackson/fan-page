Why I Love Detrtoit

Detroit is a city located in Michigan where you can see Canada across the river.I was born and raised in Detroit. I have alot of family that still live there.I Lived in Detroit for over thirty years, also I did most of my education there. The crime rate is high for an mostly all African american city, but it's a place where you can have alot of fun.
